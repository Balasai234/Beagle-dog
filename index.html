Beagle dog

The beagle is a breed of small scent hound similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare known as beagling.
Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts,the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs
in quarantine around the world.The beagle is a popular pet due to its size and good temper.
